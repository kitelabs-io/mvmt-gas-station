# Create a Gas Station

This guide will walk you through the process of creating a new gas station using the Gas Station interface.

## Steps to Create a Gas Station

1. **Visit Gas Station Interface**

- Navigate to the `Gas Station` interface in the header bar.

2. **Click on "New Station" Button**

- Look for and click on the "New Station" button, typically located in the top right corner of the interface.

3. **Submit the Registration**

- Complete and submit the form with the required information 

Congratulations! You have successfully created a gas station. You should now see your new gas station listed in the UI.

Before proceeding to the next step, use your preferred wallet to send funds to the gas station's address. This will ensure that your gas station has the necessary resources to sponsor transactions.
